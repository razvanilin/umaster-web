Early look

uMaster PC Client

uMaster comes with two sleek PC clients, for Windows and MacOS. (Linux soon)

The PC client enables you to create new activities and templates. The activities can be activated from the Web application. Once you log in on this website you will be able to view and activate all the activities created on your computer.

Test your creativity with Templates

The uMaster PC app lets you create your own templates.

You can plug in your own script that you want to run remotely. The great thing is that you can use parameters and configure the template to add fields in the interface that act as parameter inputs.

New activities can be created using the templates.

Responsive & Simple

Navigating through the app menus feels like a breeze on both the PC client and Web App.

The material design from Google offers a familliar view and experience. The activities can be launched easily from every device no matter what size the screen is.
